Service

Delta Kappa Delta is the only South Asian interest service sorority on the Texas A&M campus.

We complete 45 hours of service throughout the semester within the College Station/Bryan area.

The organization recently acquired its own street on Brentwood Dr. in College Station and our sisters meet up there twice a month to clean the area together.

Our philanthropy is child abuse awareness and prevention so we spend a lot of time educating people on campus through tabling and other events. We also volunteer with establishments across the area such as Boys and Girls Club and Voices for Children.

Every year we work together with other organizations under the Multicultural Greek Council (MGC) to volunteer at Big Event and give back to the community.
